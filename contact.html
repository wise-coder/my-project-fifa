Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot password". Enter your registered email address and we'll send you instructions to create a new password.
Why wasn't my screenshot processed?
Screenshots may not be processed if they don't meet our validation criteria. Common reasons include: unclear image quality, incorrect match format, or duplicate submissions. Please ensure your screenshot clearly shows the match statistics and try again.
How are points calculated?
Points are calculated based on various match statistics including goals scored, possession percentage, shots on target, and the match outcome (win/draw/loss). Check our "How It Works" page for detailed scoring information.
Can I appeal a ranking decision?
Yes, if you believe there was an error in your ranking or points calculation, please contact our support team with detailed information about the issue. We'll review your case and get back to you within 48 hours.
How do I report a player for cheating?
We take fair play very seriously. If you suspect another player of cheating or manipulating their scores, please contact our admin team with evidence and details about the suspicious activity.
