What We Do

FIFA Stats is a comprehensive football performance platform designed specifically for Dream League Soccer players. Our mission is to help you track your match outcomes, measure your progress over time, and compete fairly on our global leaderboard. We understand that every victory matters, and that's why we've built a system that accurately captures and validates your achievements.

Players upload their match statistics screenshots through our secure upload system, and our advanced backend analyzes each submission to calculate points based on your performance. Whether you've scored a dramatic last-minute winner or maintained possession dominance throughout the match, we ensure your accomplishments are properly recorded and rewarded on our leaderboard.

Our platform continuously tracks your personal progress, rankings, and provides notifications so you can monitor your growth as a player. From your first upload to becoming a top-ranked competitor, we're here to document your journey through the exciting world of Dream League Soccer.
