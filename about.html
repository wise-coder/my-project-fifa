About FIFA Stats

FIFA Stats is a football performance platform built to help players track match outcomes, measure progress, and compete fairly on a leaderboard.

What We Do

Players upload match-stat screenshots, and our backend analyzes them to calculate points. The system updates personal progress, rankings, and notifications so each player can monitor growth over time.

Core Features

  • Screenshot-based match verification and scoring
  • Player leaderboard and rank tracking
  • Progress analytics and match history
  • Admin announcements and moderation tools

Fair Play Goal

We focus on maintaining reliable scoring by validating uploaded screenshots and reducing invalid or unrelated submissions.
