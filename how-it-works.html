How It Works

Get started with FIFA Stats in minutes! Learn how to upload your match screenshots, earn points, and climb the leaderboard to become the ultimate Dream League Soccer champion.

1

Create Your Free Account

The first step to tracking your Dream League Soccer progress is creating a free account on FIFA Stats. Visit our registration page and provide a valid email address and choose a unique username.

Your account serves as your personal dashboard where you can:

  • Upload match screenshots
  • View your statistics and progress
  • Check your leaderboard ranking
  • Receive notifications about your achievements

Quick Tip

Choose a memorable username as it will be displayed on the public leaderboard for all to see!

2

Upload Match Screenshot

After logging into your dashboard, navigate to the Upload Match section. Here you can upload a screenshot of your Dream League Soccer match statistics. Make sure the image clearly shows:

  • Final score (goals for both teams)
  • Possession percentage
  • Shots on target
  • Match date and competition type

Supported formats include JPG, JPEG, and PNG. The maximum file size is 5MB per upload.

Best Practices

Use high-quality screenshots where all statistics are clearly visible. Avoid blurry or dark images that may affect our system's ability to read the data accurately.

3

Automatic Analysis & Scoring

Once you upload your screenshot, our advanced AI system analyzes the image to extract match statistics. The system calculates your points based on various performance metrics:

Metric Points Range Description
Match Outcome +30 to +50 Win, Draw, or Loss
Goals Scored +5 per goal Goals you scored
Possession +1 to +15 Based on possession %
Shots on Target +2 per shot Accuracy bonus
Clean Sheet +20 No goals conceded

The system validates your screenshot to ensure it's a legitimate Dream League Soccer match result, maintaining fair play for all users.

4

Earn Points & Build Your Ranking

Every match you upload contributes to your total score. Your points accumulate over time, and your ranking on the global leaderboard is determined by your total score.

The leaderboard is updated in real-time, so you can see how you stack up against other Dream League Soccer players worldwide. Aim for the top!

  • Track your progress over time
  • Compete for top positions on the leaderboard
  • Earn achievements as you improve
  • Compare stats with friends
5

Track Your Progress

Your dashboard provides comprehensive analytics about your Dream League Soccer performance. Monitor your improvement with detailed statistics including:

  • Total matches played and win rate
  • Goals scored and conceded
  • Average possession statistics
  • Performance trends over time
  • Recent match history

Stay motivated by watching your stats improve and your ranking climb week by week!

Tips for Maximizing Your Score

Win Every Match

Victories earn the most points. Focus on consistent wins rather than high-scoring draws.

Keep Clean Sheets

Defensive excellence pays off! Conceding zero goals gives you a +20 point bonus.

Score Goals

Every goal adds +5 points. High-scoring wins can significantly boost your total.

Dominate Possession

Higher possession percentages earn more points. Control the game to maximize scores.

Upload Consistently

Upload your match results regularly to build your score and track your improvement.

Play Fair

Only upload legitimate match results. Fair play ensures your achievements are meaningful!
