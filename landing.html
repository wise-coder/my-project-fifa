How to Earn Points

1

Upload Your Match Result

After playing a Dream League Soccer match, take a screenshot of your match statistics and upload it through your dashboard.

2

AI Analyzes Your Performance

Our advanced AI system automatically analyzes your screenshot to extract match statistics and calculates your points.

3

Earn Points Based on Performance

Points are awarded for: Wins (30-50 pts), Goals (+5 per goal), Possession (+1-15), Shots on Target (+2 each), Clean Sheets (+20).

4

Watch Your Ranking Grow

As you accumulate points, your position on the global leaderboard improves. Aim for consistency to reach the top!

How to Upload Screenshots

1

Create an Account

Register for a free account on FIFA Stats using your email address.

2

Navigate to Upload Section

After logging in, click on "Upload Match" in your dashboard sidebar.

3

Select Your Screenshot

Click on the upload area or drag and drop your screenshot. Supported: JPG, JPEG, PNG (Max 5MB).

4

Submit for Processing

Click the upload button and our system will analyze it within seconds!
